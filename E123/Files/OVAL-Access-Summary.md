# OVAL Access - Product Summary
**Tier Level**: Foundational Membership

## Product Overview

OVAL Access is the foundational tier of the Oval healthcare platform, providing essential access to Oval's streamlined digital healthcare experience. This modern healthcare membership is designed for the contemporary individual who wants on-demand, personalized care with clinical credibility and effortless access. More than just a clinic, Oval represents a comprehensive digital health solution that integrates telemedicine, pharmacy fulfillment, and intelligent membership plans.

## Key Tagline
*"Essential access to modern, on-demand healthcare - beautifully designed for your lifestyle."*

## Core Benefits

### 1. Access to the Oval Experience
Engage with Oval's modern, online, and comfortable platform utilizing full-stack digital health services including telemedicine and pharmacy fulfillment, all designed for on-demand and personalized care.

### 2. Plan Personalization with OvalAI
Leverage Oval's artificial intelligence to create intelligent and personalized membership plans tailored to your specific health needs and preferences.

### 3. Telemedicine/Telepharmacy
- Conduct online health assessments

- Medical professionals review your information

### 4. 24/7 Support
Continuous access to ongoing support including:
- Regular check-ins
- Necessary medication adjustments
- Direct messaging with healthcare providers around the clock

### 5. Free Discreet Shipping
If a prescription is provided, treatment ships directly to your door at no additional cost, ensuring convenience and privacy.

### 6. GLP-1 Consultation & Assessment
Provider consultation that tailors weight loss goals by pairing prescription medication with coaching and care, including:
- Health assessments
- Medical review
- GLP-1 prescription (if appropriate)
- Ongoing support

## How It Works

### Workflow Process:
1. **Enrollment**: Sign up online at https://ovalcare.com/login, by phone (1-305-939-0168), or by mail
2. **Account Creation**: Receive welcome email with portal access
3. **Treatment Selection**: Browse available treatments and select your care needs
4. **Medical Intake**: Complete dynamic health questionnaire with ID verification
5. **Provider Review**: Licensed physician reviews your case within 8 hours
6. **Prescription & Fulfillment**: Approved prescriptions sent to white-label pharmacy
7. **Delivery & Tracking**: Medication shipped with tracking to your door
8. **Ongoing Care**: Access dashboard, request refills, track shipments, and receive follow-up care

- Prescription approval based on clinical evaluation
- 8-hour physician review window

- Not a replacement for emergency medical care
