Important Information & Considerations

Telemedicine Service:

This service is provided via telemedicine and is not intended for emergency medical situations. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please call 911 or visit your nearest emergency room. All prescriptions are subject to provider approval based on clinical evaluation and medical history.

Provider Review Process:

A licensed physician will review your medical intake within an 8-hour timeframe. Approval is based on established clinical protocols and your individual health profile. Video submission may be requested for certain treatments to ensure proper diagnosis and care planning.

Identity Verification Required:

All members must upload a clear photograph of a valid government-issued ID for identity verification. This ensures the safety and security of all prescriptions and protects member information. Treatment availability may vary based on individual medical history, current conditions, and state regulations.
