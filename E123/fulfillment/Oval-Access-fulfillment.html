Getting Started with OVAL

Your Healthcare Journey Starts Here

Getting started with OVAL is simple and takes just minutes. Follow these steps to begin your personalized healthcare experience:

Step 1: Access Your OVAL Portal

Visit https://ovalcare.com/login to access your patient portal. You should have received a welcome email with your account credentials. If you haven't received your welcome email within 24 hours of enrollment, please contact support.

  • Enter your email address and temporary password
  • Create a secure, permanent password
  • Verify your identity with government-issued ID
  • Complete your basic profile information

Important: Identity verification is required for all prescriptions to ensure your safety and security. Please have a clear photo of your driver's license or government ID ready.

Step 2: Browse Available Treatments

Explore the treatments and services available through your OVAL Access membership. Our platform covers multiple healthcare categories:

Primary Care

General consultations, symptom assessment, treatment planning

Weight Management

GLP-1 consultation, personalized weight loss planning

Mental Health

Initial assessment, treatment consultation, ongoing support

General Wellness

Preventive care, lifestyle recommendations, health monitoring

Step 3: Complete Your Health Assessment

Once you've selected a treatment, you'll complete a dynamic health questionnaire designed specifically for your condition. This assessment helps our licensed physicians understand your health history and create a personalized treatment plan.

  • Answer questions about your medical history and current health
  • Provide information about medications and allergies
  • Upload required photos or videos (if applicable)
  • Submit your completed assessment for review

The health assessment typically takes 5-10 minutes to complete. Be thorough and honest—accurate information ensures you receive the most appropriate care.

Step 4: Provider Review & Approval

A licensed, board-certified physician will review your health assessment within 8 hours. Our providers follow evidence-based protocols to ensure you receive safe, effective treatment.

What Happens During Review:
  1. Physician evaluates your medical history and current health status
  2. Treatment appropriateness is determined based on clinical guidelines
  3. If approved, prescription is sent to white-label pharmacy
  4. If additional information needed, provider will message you directly

You'll receive a notification via email and in your patient portal once your provider has completed the review. If your treatment is approved, your prescription will automatically be sent for fulfillment.

Step 5: Prescription Fulfillment & Delivery

If your provider approves a prescription, it will be sent to our partner pharmacy for fulfillment. Your medication will be shipped directly to your door with free discreet shipping.

  • Packaging: All shipments are discreet with no indication of medical contents
  • Tracking: Real-time shipment tracking available in your patient portal
  • Delivery Time: Most orders arrive within 5-7 business days
  • Signature: Signature may be required for certain medications

You'll receive email and portal notifications at each step: prescription sent to pharmacy, order shipped, and delivery confirmation.

Step 6: Ongoing Care & Follow-Up

Your healthcare journey doesn't end with delivery. OVAL provides continuous support to ensure your treatment success:

  • Regular Check-Ins: Your provider will follow up on your progress
  • Medication Adjustments: Dosing can be modified as needed
  • Direct Messaging: Ask questions anytime via secure messaging
  • Refill Management: Request refills through your portal when needed
  • 24/7 Support: Access to healthcare professionals around the clock

Ready to experience modern healthcare? Here's your quick reference guide to using OVAL Access:

GLP-1 Weight Management Consultation

Personalized Weight Loss Support

Your OVAL Access membership includes a comprehensive GLP-1 consultation and assessment. This provider consultation tailors weight loss goals by pairing prescription medication with professional coaching and ongoing care.

What's Included in Your GLP-1 Consultation:

1. Comprehensive Health Assessment

Complete medical evaluation including weight history, lifestyle factors, previous weight loss attempts, and current health conditions that may affect treatment.

2. Medical Review by Licensed Provider

Board-certified physician reviews your assessment to determine if GLP-1 medication is appropriate based on your health profile and weight loss goals.

3. GLP-1 Prescription (if appropriate)

If clinically appropriate, your provider will prescribe GLP-1 medication to help manage appetite and support weight loss efforts.

4. Personalized Coaching & Support

Receive ongoing guidance on nutrition, exercise, lifestyle modifications, and medication management to maximize your weight loss success.

5. Regular Check-Ins

Your provider monitors your progress and adjusts treatment as needed. Check in regularly to discuss results, side effects, and any concerns.

How to Start Your Weight Loss Journey

Ready to begin your transformation with GLP-1 medications? Here's your simple step-by-step guide:

  1. Log into your OVAL portal at https://ovalcare.com/login
  2. Select "Weight Management" from available treatments
  3. Complete the GLP-1 consultation questionnaire (about 10 minutes)
  4. Submit your assessment for physician review
  5. Receive provider decision within 8 hours
  6. If approved, medication ships free to your door
  7. Begin your personalized weight loss program with ongoing support

Note: GLP-1 medications are prescription-only and require provider approval. Not all patients will qualify based on medical history, current health status, and clinical guidelines. Your provider will discuss all options during the consultation process.

Need Help? Your dedicated support team is ready to guide you through every step. Contact them via the patient portal or call the priority line for personalized assistance.

Important Information & Disclaimers

Telemedicine Service - Not for Emergencies

OVAL provides healthcare services via telemedicine. This service is not intended for emergency medical situations. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please call 911 or visit your nearest emergency room immediately. OVAL is designed for non-urgent consultations, ongoing care management, and prescription services.

Prescription Approval Process

All prescriptions are subject to provider approval based on clinical evaluation, medical history, and current health status. Not all treatment requests will be approved. Providers follow evidence-based protocols and may request additional information or decline treatment if not clinically appropriate. Video submission or additional documentation may be required for certain treatments.

Provider Review Timeline

Licensed physicians review medical assessments within an 8-hour timeframe. This is a maximum timeframe; many reviews are completed sooner. Review times may be longer during high-volume periods or weekends. You will receive notification once your review is complete.

Identity Verification Required

All members must upload a clear photograph of a valid government-issued ID (driver's license, passport, or state ID) for identity verification. This is required by law to ensure prescription safety and prevent fraud. Your information is protected with HIPAA-compliant encryption and security measures.

Treatment Availability & Limitations

Treatment availability varies based on individual medical history, current conditions, state regulations, and provider clinical judgment. Some medications may not be available in certain states. Age restrictions, contraindications, and drug interactions may limit treatment options. Your provider will discuss alternatives if your requested treatment is not appropriate.

Privacy & Security

All medical information is protected under HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act). Your health data is encrypted and stored securely. Medications are shipped in discreet packaging with no external indication of medical contents. We never share your information without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

Shipping & Delivery

Free shipping is included for all prescriptions. Delivery times typically range from 5-7 business days but may vary based on location and carrier delays. Signature may be required for certain controlled medications. OVAL is not responsible for delays caused by weather, carrier issues, or incorrect shipping addresses. Please ensure your shipping information is accurate in your profile.
