Important Information for Specialty Medications

Specialist Approval Required

All specialty medications require approval from board-certified specialists. Not all treatment requests will be approved. Eligibility depends on medical history, current health status, contraindications, drug interactions, and clinical protocols. Your specialist will discuss alternative options if your requested treatment is not appropriate for you.

Condition-Specific Assessments

Each specialty category requires a unique medical assessment tailored to that condition. Assessments may request detailed information, medical history, photos, or video consultations to ensure accurate diagnosis and appropriate treatment. Complete all questions thoroughly and honestly for best results.

Side Effects & Monitoring

All medications can cause side effects. Your specialist will discuss common side effects during the consultation process. Report any unusual or severe reactions immediately through secure messaging or by calling our support line. Regular monitoring may be required for certain medications, especially mental health prescriptions.

State Regulations & Availability

Medication availability varies by state due to telemedicine regulations and controlled substance laws. Some treatments may not be available in your state. Your specialist will inform you of any limitations and discuss alternative options if applicable.

Not for Emergencies

OVAL provides specialty care via telemedicine for non-urgent conditions. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, suicidal thoughts, severe allergic reactions, or any medical emergency, call 911 or visit your nearest emergency room immediately. Our 24/7 messaging is for routine questions and non-urgent concerns only.

Treatment Expectations

Results vary by individual and condition. Hair loss treatments may take 3-6 months to show results. Mental health medications often require 4-6 weeks to reach full effectiveness. ED medications work within hours but require sexual stimulation. Your specialist will set realistic expectations and timelines for your specific treatment.
